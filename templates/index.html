Player Valuability Ratings for
OUA Student Athletes

The Player Valuability Rating is an all-in-one rating that considers every single one of a player's contributions to their team and generates a number that gives an accurate representation of how valuable they are to a team. PVR strives to measures how valuable a player is to their team by taking a player's statistics for a single game and identifying how impactful he was to that said team.
